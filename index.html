What?

Limerick Institute of Technology has a postgraduate course in the area of Multimedia. The HDip in Creative Multimedia Programming is a popular choice for mature students who have ann interest in the area of IT and Multimedia. The course attracts creative individuals who also want to learn skills in programming. It also attracts programming individuals who want to explore a more creative area of IT. The mulitmedia sector of the IT industry is an area that is growing rapidly. This course covers a range of disciplines in IT as it is creative yet technical with subjects like Interactive Multimedia Design, Web Development and Programming.

Where?

Limerick Institute of Technology (LIT) in located in Limerick city in Munster, Ireland. Limerick is a vibrant city with a long history and great sporting culture. The college itself is located beside the home of Munster rugby, Thomond Park. The college offers a range of undergratuate and postgraduate courses in many diciplines and also covers many areas of IT.
